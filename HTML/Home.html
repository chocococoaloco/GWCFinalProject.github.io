Climate Change Misconceptions

Climate change is defined as any significant long-term change in the expected patterns of average weather of a region (or the whole Earth) over a significant period of time. Climate change is about abnormal variations to the climate, and the effects of these variations on other parts of the Earth.

Because climate change is a topic that is talked about a lot, there are a lot of misconceptions spreading around, leading to the spread false information. It is super important to know and understand how and why climate change is happening, and what we can do before it worsens.
